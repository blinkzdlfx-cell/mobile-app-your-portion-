ROLES & PERMISSIONS PLAN - Your Portion App

============================================
ADMIN
============================================
- Full CRUD on all properties and projects
- Approve/reject seller listings
- Approve/reject kingdom projects
- Manage user roles (promote to trusted member, seller)
- View all transactions and activity
- Moderate reviews/ratings
- Platform settings & content management

============================================
SELLER
============================================
- Create, edit, delete own property listings
- View own listings' analytics (views, saves)
- Respond to buyer inquiries on own listings
- Create kingdom projects
- List up to X properties (limit)
- View buyer interest metrics

============================================
BUYER
============================================
- Browse all properties and projects
- Save/favorite properties
- Contact seller via their listed social media / contact (WhatsApp, phone, email)
- View property details and location
- Rate and review properties (after engagement)
- Submit kingdom project donations
- Access learning library content
